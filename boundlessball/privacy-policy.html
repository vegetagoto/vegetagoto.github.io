Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Thank you for playing Boundless Ball.

Boundless Ball is a simple offline arcade game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information Collection

Boundless Ball does not collect, store, process, or share any personal information.

The game does not require user registration and does not ask for personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Advertising

Boundless Ball does not display advertisements.

Analytics

Boundless Ball does not use analytics services and does not track user activity.

Internet Connection

The game can be played without an internet connection and does not transmit gameplay data to any server.

Children's Privacy

Because no personal information is collected, Boundless Ball does not knowingly collect information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If future versions of Boundless Ball introduce features that require data collection, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
